# Network Map

The network map provides a visual topology view of your managed devices, WAN connections, and network relationships.

## What You Can See

- **Device locations** — your managed firewalls positioned geographically or logically
- **WAN connections** — ISP links and their status
- **Network relationships** — how devices connect to each other (VPN tunnels, HA pairs)
- **Status indicators** — device health and connection status at a glance

## Use Cases

- **Quick situational awareness** — see your entire fleet's status visually
- **Outage identification** — spot affected devices and WAN links during incidents
- **Network planning** — understand the topology before making changes
- **Client presentations** — visual overview for non-technical stakeholders
