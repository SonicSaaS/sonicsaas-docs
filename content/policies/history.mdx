# Policy History

The policy history page provides an audit trail of all policy changes across your fleet.

## What Gets Tracked

Every policy change is recorded with full context:

- **What changed** — which policy module, which settings, old vs. new values
- **Who changed it** — which team member made the change
- **When** — timestamp of the change
- **Scope** — whether the change applies globally, to an organization, or to a specific device

## Why Policy History Matters

Policy history supports several compliance and operational needs:

- **Audit trail** — demonstrate to auditors when policies were established and modified
- **Troubleshooting** — understand why a device's compliance status changed
- **Change management** — review who authorized policy changes and when
- **Drift investigation** — correlate policy changes with device compliance changes

## Filtering and Navigation

You can filter policy history by time range, policy module, scope level, or team member. Click through to the relevant device or policy to see the current state.
