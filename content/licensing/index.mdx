# Licensing

The licensing page provides fleet-wide visibility into license status, expiration dates, and support coverage for your managed firewalls.

## What You Can See

- **License status** — which devices have active licenses and which are expired or expiring
- **Expiration tracking** — upcoming expirations so you can plan renewals
- **Support tier** — the level of support coverage for each device
- **Warranty status** — warranty expiration and end-of-life information
- **Service entitlements** — which security services are licensed per device

## Why License Tracking Matters

Expired licenses can disable critical security services on your firewalls. Fleet-wide license visibility helps you:

- **Prevent security gaps** — renew before licenses expire and services stop working
- **Plan budgets** — see all upcoming renewals in one view
- **Identify waste** — find licenses that are paid for but not actively used
- **Track end-of-life** — plan hardware refresh cycles for EOL devices

## MySonicWall Integration

When the [MySonicWall integration](/integrations/mysonicwall) is configured, license data is automatically synced from the cloud portal, keeping your licensing information current.
