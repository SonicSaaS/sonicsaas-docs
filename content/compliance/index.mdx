# Compliance Dashboard

The compliance dashboard provides a unified view of your fleet's compliance status across all policy modules.

## What You Can See

- **Overall compliance score** — percentage of devices meeting all applicable policies
- **Per-module breakdown** — compliance status for firmware, security services, local users, backups, and passwords
- **Non-compliant devices** — devices that need attention, grouped by violation type
- **Trends** — how your compliance posture has changed over time

## How Compliance Is Measured

The platform evaluates each device against all active policies:

1. **Firmware** — is the device running at or above the target version?
2. **Security services** — are required security features enabled?
3. **Local users** — are all accounts authorized and properly configured?
4. **Backups** — does the device have a recent backup?
5. **Passwords** — do admin passwords meet policy requirements?

A device is considered fully compliant only when it passes all applicable checks.

## Taking Action

From the compliance dashboard, you can:

- Click through to non-compliant devices to investigate
- Launch remediation operations (firmware updates, backups) in bulk
- Export compliance data for audit reporting
