# Benchmark

The benchmark page lets you compare and group devices across your fleet to identify patterns, outliers, and standardization opportunities.

## What You Can See

- **Fleet comparison** — compare devices by model, firmware version, configuration, and security posture
- **Grouping** — organize devices by model family, firmware version, organization, or custom criteria
- **Outliers** — identify devices that deviate from the fleet norm

## Use Cases

- **Standardization** — find devices running different configurations than their peers
- **Upgrade planning** — group devices by model to plan firmware update campaigns
- **Capacity planning** — compare resource utilization across device types
- **Compliance** — verify consistent security configurations across similar devices
