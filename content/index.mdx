# SonicSaaS Documentation

Welcome to the SonicSaaS documentation. SonicSaaS is a secure fleet management platform for MSPs that wraps SonicWall firewalls (and other vendor tools) with RBAC, immutable audit trails, encrypted credentials, and unified policy enforcement.
