# General Settings

General settings control your team's identity, organization structure, and notification preferences.

## Team Settings

- **Team name** — the display name for your team
- **Subscription** — your current plan and billing status

## Organization Management

Organizations let you group devices by client, site, or any logical grouping. From general settings you can:

- Create new organizations
- Rename or archive existing organizations
- View device counts per organization

Organizations are used throughout the platform for filtering, access restrictions, and policy scoping. See [Roles & Permissions](/security/roles-and-permissions) for how organization restrictions work.

## Notification Preferences

Configure how and when you receive notifications:

- Email notification preferences (requires [email integration](/integrations/email))
- Which event types trigger notifications
- Notification frequency and batching
