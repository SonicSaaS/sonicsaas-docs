# Operational Settings

Operational settings control how SonicSaaS interacts with your managed firewalls — polling frequency, concurrency limits, timeouts, and other parameters that affect fleet operations.

## What You Can Configure

- **Poll intervals** — how often the platform checks device status and pulls configuration data
- **Concurrency limits** — how many devices can be operated on simultaneously during fleet operations
- **Connection timeouts** — how long to wait for a device to respond before marking it unreachable
- **Operation defaults** — default settings for backup, firmware, and other fleet operations

## Team-Overridable Settings

Operational settings use a layered configuration model. The platform provides sensible defaults, but your team can override them to match your environment:

- **Platform defaults** — conservative settings suitable for most deployments
- **Team overrides** — your customized values that take precedence

## Tuning Recommendations

### Poll Intervals

- **More frequent polling** gives you fresher data and faster change detection, but increases load on your devices and network
- **Less frequent polling** reduces load but means changes take longer to appear in the platform

### Concurrency

- **Higher concurrency** completes fleet operations faster but may overwhelm devices or network
- **Lower concurrency** is safer for large fleets or devices on slower connections

### Timeouts

- Increase timeouts if you manage devices on slow or high-latency connections
- The default timeouts work well for LAN-connected devices
