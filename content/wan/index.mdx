# WAN Overview

The WAN section provides fleet-wide visibility into your internet connections, dual-WAN configurations, and ISP health across all managed firewalls.

## What You Can See

- **ISP inventory** — all internet service providers across your fleet with connection status
- **Dual-WAN status** — which devices have multiple WAN links and their failover configuration
- **Connection health** — uptime, latency, and availability for each WAN link
- **Geographic information** — ISP name, public IP, and location for each connection

## ISP Management

The WAN overview aggregates ISP information across your fleet, letting you:

- See which ISPs serve the most devices
- Identify ISP-related outages affecting multiple sites
- Track WAN link health over time
- Navigate to individual device details for troubleshooting

Clicking on an ISP shows all devices connected through that provider, along with health metrics and historical data.

## Failover Monitoring

For devices with dual-WAN configurations, you can monitor:

- Which WAN link is primary versus backup
- Failover events and their duration
- Link utilization to plan capacity
