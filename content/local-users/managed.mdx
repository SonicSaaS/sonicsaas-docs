# Managed Users

Managed users are local accounts on your firewalls that SonicSaaS actively manages. This gives you centralized control over who has access to your devices.

## How Managed Users Work

When you designate a local user account as "managed," SonicSaaS takes responsibility for:

- Tracking the account's existence across devices
- Monitoring its configuration against your policies
- Flagging changes that weren't made through the platform

## Benefits

- **Centralized visibility** — see all managed accounts across your fleet in one place
- **Change detection** — know when someone modifies a managed account directly on the device
- **Compliance tracking** — verify managed accounts meet your password and MFA policies
- **Audit trail** — all changes to managed accounts are logged
