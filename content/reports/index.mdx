# Reports

The reports page provides compliance, operational, and fleet health reports that you can generate and export.

## Report Types

- **Compliance reports** — policy compliance status across your fleet, suitable for audit evidence
- **Operational reports** — backup coverage, firmware status, operation history summaries
- **Fleet health reports** — device status, connectivity trends, security posture overview

## Use Cases

- **Audit preparation** — generate compliance evidence for SOC 2 or other frameworks
- **Client reporting** — produce reports for MSP clients showing their fleet health
- **Executive summaries** — high-level security posture for management review
- **Trend analysis** — track fleet health over time
