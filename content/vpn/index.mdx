# VPN Overview

The VPN section gives you fleet-wide visibility into IPSec and SSL-VPN tunnel status across all your managed firewalls.

## What You Can See

From a single dashboard, you can monitor:

- **Tunnel status** — which tunnels are up, down, or in a degraded state
- **Tunnel inventory** — a complete list of all VPN tunnels across your fleet
- **Connection metrics** — traffic and session data for active tunnels

## VPN Types

SonicSaaS monitors two types of VPN connections:

### IPSec (Site-to-Site)

IPSec tunnels connect two networks securely over the internet. These are typically used for:

- Branch office connectivity
- Datacenter interconnects
- Partner network connections

See [IPSec Tunnels](/vpn/ipsec) for details.

### SSL-VPN (Remote Access)

SSL-VPN portals provide remote access for individual users. These are typically used for:

- Remote worker access
- Vendor access portals
- Emergency access

See [SSL-VPN](/vpn/ssl-vpn) for details.

## VPN Compliance

The VPN compliance page evaluates your tunnel configurations against security best practices — encryption standards, perfect forward secrecy, certificate expiration, and more.

See [VPN Compliance](/vpn/compliance) for details.
