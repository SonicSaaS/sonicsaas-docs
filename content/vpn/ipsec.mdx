# IPSec Tunnels

The IPSec tunnels page provides a fleet-wide view of all site-to-site VPN tunnels across your managed firewalls.

## What You Can See

- **Tunnel status** — up, down, or negotiating
- **Peers** — remote gateway addresses and identifiers
- **Encryption** — algorithms and key exchange methods in use
- **Traffic** — bytes and packets transferred through each tunnel
- **Device** — which firewall owns each tunnel

## Fleet-Wide Visibility

Unlike managing tunnels device-by-device, this view aggregates all IPSec tunnels across your entire fleet. You can quickly identify:

- Tunnels that are down and may need attention
- Tunnels using weak or outdated encryption
- Devices with the most active tunnels
- Peer relationships across your network

## Filtering

You can filter tunnels by status, device, organization, encryption method, or peer address to focus on specific areas of concern.
