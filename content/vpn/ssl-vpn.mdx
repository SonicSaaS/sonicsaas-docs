# SSL-VPN

The SSL-VPN page provides fleet-wide visibility into SSL-VPN portal configurations and user access across your managed firewalls.

## What You Can See

- **Portal configurations** — SSL-VPN portal settings on each device
- **User profiles** — who has access to each portal
- **License usage** — how many SSL-VPN licenses are in use versus available
- **Session data** — active and historical SSL-VPN sessions

## Use Cases

SSL-VPN portals are commonly used for:

- **Remote workers** — secure access to internal resources from any location
- **Vendor access** — time-limited portals for third-party support
- **Emergency access** — backup connectivity when IPSec tunnels are down

## Fleet-Wide Management

The fleet view lets you see all SSL-VPN configurations across your managed devices in one place, making it easy to:

- Identify devices with unused or over-provisioned SSL-VPN licenses
- Review who has remote access across your fleet
- Ensure consistent portal configurations across similar devices
